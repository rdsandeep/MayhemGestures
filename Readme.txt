Kinect Gestures for Mayhem

This is a module for the Mayhem application(http://mayhem.codeplex.com)
It consist of two events(Swipe Left and Swipe Right)
To use the events, copy the contents of bin/Debug folder into
C:\Program Files (x86)\Outercurve\Mayhem
Then launch Mayhem, plug in the Kinect sensor(either Kinect for XBOX 360
or Kinect for Windows) into the PC and also connect it to the power supply.
And then go ahead and create event-reaction combinations.
